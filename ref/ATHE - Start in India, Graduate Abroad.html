How It Works

Study in India. Graduate Abroad.

The RVS 2+1 Pathway lets you complete the first two years in India and progress to an International University for your final year. Globally recognised. Academically rigorous. Financially smart.

Institutions & Academic Partners

Institutions & Academic Partners

This academic pathway is delivered through a strong collaboration between recognised education providers and global universities

The ATHE is an Ofqual-regulated awarding organisation based in the UK, providing Internationally recognised qualifications aligned with the advanced higher education standards of the country. In India, the academic programme is delivered by Transform Tech, under the ATHE framework, through the RVS College of Arts & Science in Coimbatore, under the RVS Educational Trust. Students study year 1 and 2 at RVS and progress to our partner universities in any of the 7 countries.
